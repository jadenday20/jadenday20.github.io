This Site

String Instruments and Orchestras is an informational website whose purpose is to educate those who are new to the orchestra world.

In this website we will go over many of the most important aspects of string insturments and orchestras. We will go over information about each of the four instruments, and about orchestras in general. We will also discuss the most fundamental techniques as well as some of the more advanced ones.

Get ready to learn all about what makes the beautiful music you know and love! If you finish reading this and you want more then no need to fear; The internet is full of great sources of knowledge:)

About the Author

Jaden Day is from Lindon Utah where he grew up with his older brother and six younger brothers. He started playing the violin at the age of nine and took violin lessons from 4th grade until 8th grade. Starting in 9th grade, Jaden played in his school orchestra where he later learned to play the cello. Jaden is currently a freshman at BYU-Idaho where he studies software engineering.
