String Instruments and Orchestras Site Plan

Jaden Day

Overview

Purpose

To educate people about the concepts of string instruments and orchestras.

Audience

This site is inteded for anyone who is new to string instruments and orchestras. My site will help those who know little or nothing about string instruments and orchestras to discover what it is all about. It will help them learn about basic things like sections in an orchestra, the parts within an instrument and so on.

My Pitch

Have you ever been curious about orchestral music? Have you ever wanted to know more about just what exactly goes into the beautiful music you love? My name is Jaden Day, and I am a violinist. My website will break down all of the many components that make up a string orchestra, from instruments to techniques to vocabulary. My goal is to help you better understand the orchestra experience one concept at a time.
